Mac N' Cheese

This is a cheesy pasta dish perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Shells
  • Cheese (Cheddar and Mozarella)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sweet Peppers
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs

Method

  1. Heat a pan
  2. Melt butter in pan
  3. Add Onion and sweet peppers
  4. Add Garlic and fry until fragrant
  5. Add Flour
  6. Add Milk
  7. Add boiled pasta
  8. Add Cheese
  9. Mix until combined
  10. Top with more cheese and bread crumbs
